A Little About Me

Life has interesting way of lighting a path you never thought you'd find yourself on.

Growing up in a suburb of metro Detroit, I was never one to be found behind a computer screen. As the middle child in a family of 6, I spent most of my time outdoors playing sports or behind a drumset playing music. It wasn't until I moved to Austin TX in my early twenties that discovered an unforeseen interest in computers and its benefits in my context. I was thrust into the workforce with a limited skillset in the ever-growing technological landscape. To advance, I dove headfirst into learning as much as I could about whatever relevant system or software my employer was using. From anything to excel to programming or particular management software, I devoted myself to gain as much knowledge about whatever application I was tasked with to ensure advancement and sustainability in whatever work environment I found myself in. In seeking to provide value to my employer and my team, I discovered a new found passion for design and development that I otherwise would not have had.
